Frequently Asked Questions

Is ThyroScan AI a diagnostic tool?

No, ThyroScan AI is a screening and risk assessment tool for educational and research purposes. It is not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical advice.

How accurate is the prediction model?

Our model achieves 83% accuracy on test datasets, with 87% sensitivity and 79% specificity. The model was trained on 300,000+ medical records and validated through cross-validation.

Is my medical data secure?

Yes, all data is anonymized and stored securely. We follow HIPAA compliance guidelines and use encryption for data transmission and storage. No personal health information is retained.

Can I use this for research purposes?

Absolutely! ThyroScan AI is developed as an academic research project. Researchers can use the platform for educational purposes and cite it in their research with proper attribution.

What technology stack is used?

We use FastAPI for backend, MongoDB for database, Scikit-learn for ML models, and modern web technologies (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript) for the frontend with Plotly.js for visualizations.

Is there any cost to use ThyroScan AI?

No, ThyroScan AI is completely free to use. As a university project, our goal is to make advanced thyroid screening accessible to everyone for educational purposes.
